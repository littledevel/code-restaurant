Dubai Chocolate Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 150g/5ozs kataifi pastry, chopped and separated
  2. 60g/2ozs butter
  3. 360g/12.7ozs pistachio paste
  4. 20g/0.8oz tahini
  5. Fat pinch salt
  6. 15g/0.5oz pink chocolate (I used Wilton red candy melts)
  7. 1/2 teaspoon coconut oil
  8. 15g/0.5oz green chocolate (I used Wilton green candy melts)
  9. 1/2 teaspoon coconut oil
  10. 500g/1.1lbs dark or milk chocolate
